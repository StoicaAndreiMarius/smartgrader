About SmartGrader

SmartGrader is an automated multiple-choice test management and grading system designed for educational institutions. It streamlines the entire testing workflow from test creation to automated grading using advanced Optical Mark Recognition (OMR) technology.

Built with modern web technologies and powered by AI, SmartGrader eliminates the tedious work of manual grading, allowing educators to focus on teaching while providing students with instant feedback on their performance.

Key Capabilities

  • AI-Powered Question Generation: Leverages Anthropic's Claude API to generate high-quality test questions
  • Computer Vision-Based Grading: Uses OpenCV for accurate bubble detection and answer recognition
  • Instant Feedback: Students receive immediate results upon submission
  • Flexible Grading: Supports both single and multiple correct answers with partial credit options
  • Secure Sharing: Cryptographically secure share codes for test distribution
  • Comprehensive Analytics: Detailed statistics and CSV export for further analysis

Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone. All participants in the SmartGrader community are expected to adhere to the following code of conduct.

Our Standards

  • Be Respectful: Treat all users, contributors, and community members with respect and professionalism
  • Be Inclusive: Welcome people of all backgrounds and identities
  • Be Constructive: Provide helpful feedback and criticism in a positive manner
  • Be Honest: Maintain academic integrity; do not share test answers or help others cheat
  • Respect Privacy: Do not share other users' personal information without permission

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind
  • Academic dishonesty, including sharing answers or test content without authorization
  • Attempting to circumvent security measures or access unauthorized data
  • Spamming, trolling, or disruptive behavior
  • Violating the privacy of other users

Violations of this code of conduct may result in account suspension or termination. If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to your system administrator.

Academic Integrity

SmartGrader is designed to support fair and honest assessment. Users must:

  • Students: Complete all tests independently using only authorized materials
  • Students: Not share test questions, answers, or access codes without teacher permission
  • Students: Submit only their own work
  • Teachers: Ensure tests are secure and accessible only to enrolled students
  • Teachers: Protect the confidentiality of student grades and performance data
  • Teachers: Use AI-generated questions responsibly and review them for accuracy

Academic dishonesty undermines the educational process and will not be tolerated. SmartGrader provides tools to help maintain test security, including unique share codes and submission tracking.

Privacy & Data Security

We take privacy and data security seriously:

  • User Data: Account information is stored securely and used only for authentication and test management
  • Test Results: Only the test creator (teacher) and the student can view individual test results
  • Submission Images: Answer sheet images are stored securely and accessible only to authorized users
  • Share Codes: Test access codes are cryptographically secure and unique to each test
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Student data is never shared with third parties without explicit consent

For detailed information about data handling practices, please consult your institution's privacy policy or contact your system administrator.
